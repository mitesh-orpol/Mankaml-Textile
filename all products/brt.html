BRT

BRT polyester yarn refers to "Bright" polyester yarn, which is a type of polyester fiber known for its shiny and lustrous appearance. Polyester is a synthetic fiber derived from coal, air, water, and petroleum.

It is widely used in textiles due to its durability, resistance to shrinking and stretching, quick drying, and ease of washing. BRT polyester yarn is particularly popular in applications where a glossy and vibrant finish is desired, such as in clothing, home textiles, and industrial fabrics.

Utilized in applications like automotive interiors, seat belts, and other industrial fabrics where durability and appearance are important.

Introducing our BRT Polyester Yarn, an exceptional choice for a wide range of crafting projects. Made from 100% premium polyester fibers, this yarn is designed to provide superior strength, smoothness, and a vibrant finish.
